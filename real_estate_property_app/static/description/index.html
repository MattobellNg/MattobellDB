Sale of Real Estate Property Management

Real Estate Management of Sales Property with User/Partner/Agent/Broker Sales Commissions Management

This app provides features to allow your customers or visitors to request a sales quote from your website shops/eCommerce for the property you are selling and also allows you to manage sales commission by Sales Order / Customer Invoice / Customer Payment based options to Internal Users and External Partners / Agents / Brokers.

Main Features:

  • This app allows you to publish your properties on a website shop (by creating product in the backend as property) and allow your customer/visitor on the website to get quotes for that property.
  • This app supports installment to be shown on the website using product attributes which you have to configure in the backend on the product form.
  • Your customer/visitor can view your property location on the google maps on the website shop page and also they can download documents you have uploaded on product forms like Specifications, Payment plans, Plans of building, design, and model documents. By clicking on a get quote they can fill in details and request for quotes from your company.
  • This app allows you to create installments based on the number of installments configured on the product form. This will add a button on the Sales order form to create installments in a single click.
  • Sale of Real Estate Property Management has the feature of Sales Commission so to get more details about sales commission you check the below link.
    Get more detail on sale commission then click https://www.odoo.com/apps/modules/15.0/sales_commission_external_user
